Doone Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

Doone is a productivity and focus app designed to help users create tasks, start focus sessions, reduce distractions, and build momentum.

This Privacy Policy explains how Doone collects, uses, stores, and shares information.

Developer Information

App name: Doone
Developer: Snechar
Contact: Use the developer contact email listed on Doone's Google Play Store listing.

Information We Collect

Doone is built as a local-first app. Most task and productivity data is stored on your device.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Doone may store the following information locally:

  • Tasks you create
  • Task titles, subtasks, reminders, recurring schedules, and completion history
  • Focus sessions and focus presets
  • Blocked app selections used for Focus Mode
  • XP, rewards, streaks, themes, and app settings
  • Profile display name and optional profile picture selected by you
  • Reminder preferences and notification settings

This information is stored on your device and is not uploaded to a Doone backend server.

App Blocking and Installed Apps

If you use Focus Blocking, Doone may access information about apps installed on your device or apps you choose to block. This is used only to let you select distracting apps and enable blocking during focus sessions.

Doone does not sell this information.

Notifications

Doone may use local notifications to remind you about tasks, recurring tasks, scheduled focus sessions, or focus-related actions. These notifications are generated on your device.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Doone may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage, improve the product, and diagnose crashes or technical issues.

This may include information such as:

  • App opens
  • Onboarding completion
  • Task or focus feature usage
  • Paywall views or purchase flow events
  • Crash logs
  • Device type, operating system version, app version, and general diagnostic information

Purchases and Subscriptions

Doone may use RevenueCat and Google Play Billing to manage purchases, subscriptions, free trials, and premium access.

Payment processing is handled by Google Play. Doone does not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

How We Use Information

  • Provide task, focus, reminder, and app-blocking features
  • Save your preferences and progress
  • Send local reminders and notifications
  • Manage premium access and subscriptions
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Understand general product usage
  • Detect and fix crashes or bugs

How We Share Information

Doone does not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the app:

  • Google Firebase, for analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat, for subscription and purchase management
  • Google Play Billing, for payment processing

Data Retention

Data stored locally in Doone remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, or uninstall the app.

Analytics, crash, and purchase-related data may be retained by Firebase, RevenueCat, or Google Play according to their own retention policies.

Data Deletion

You can delete locally stored Doone data by deleting tasks, changing settings, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app.

If you want to request deletion of data associated with analytics, diagnostics, or purchases where technically possible, use the developer contact email listed on Doone's Google Play Store listing.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect information handled by the app. However, no app, device, or internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Doone is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

If you use Doone outside your country of residence, your information may be processed by third-party service providers in other countries, depending on the services used, such as Firebase, RevenueCat, and Google Play.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, use the developer contact email listed on Doone's Google Play Store listing.
