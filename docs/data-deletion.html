Doone Data Deletion Request

Developer: SleepyCatzTeam

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This page explains how users of Doone can request deletion of data associated with the app.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of data associated with Doone, email us at SleepyCatzTeam@gmail.com.

Please include:

  • The app name: Doone
  • A short message saying that you want your Doone data deleted
  • Any purchase or support details needed to identify your request, if relevant
Email deletion request

Data stored on your device

Doone is local-first. Tasks, subtasks, focus presets, reminders, recurring schedules, progress, streaks, settings, profile display name, and optional profile picture are primarily stored on your device.

You can delete local Doone data at any time by deleting tasks inside the app, clearing the app's storage from Android settings, or uninstalling the app.

Data we can request to delete

Where technically possible, we can request deletion or removal of data associated with analytics, diagnostics, support, and purchase-related records processed through services used by Doone, such as Firebase, RevenueCat, and Google Play Billing.

Data that may be kept

Some records may be retained when required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, security, accounting, or platform compliance reasons. Google Play purchase records may also be retained by Google according to Google's own policies.

Retention period

We aim to respond to deletion requests within 30 days. Some third-party systems may retain backup, diagnostic, or transaction records for a limited period according to their own retention policies.

Privacy policy

You can read Doone's Privacy Policy at https://snechar.github.io/doone-legal/privacy.html.
